Competitions

The creation of competitions (or of activities) is especially thought-out for the educative use of the program. This functionality him allows to select a figure group and limit the game this group.

To create a competition, selects the menu option Competition->New competition. They accede to the screen of creation of competitions this way. The available figures of the current game level are in the left zone of the screen. To add a figure to the competition, select the name of shape (clik on his name) and add her next pressing the > > button. With east procedure can add all the figures that wants. To eliminate a figure of them selected, click on his name and next pressing the erase button( ).

To save the competition data, press the button . At any moment he can recover a competition using the button and proceed to his modification or revision.

Other possibility is to let that the program selects the competition figures at random. I press the button and the program will ask for the number of figures that he must select for the competition. Next, they will proceed to select them randomly.

To play with the figures of a competition,  is necessary he selecting the menu option Competition->Loads competition. He will must select a file of corresponding competition with the form and current game level.
