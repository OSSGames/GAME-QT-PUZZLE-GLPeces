Game description

To play, he selects the kind of game (menu Game->Kind of game) and the level (menu Play->game Level) Select a figure calling on his name the tree of figures. When doing it, he will be able to display the figure in the game zone (if they have selected the childlike, novice or initiated level)

The game starts when moving some of the pieces. He begins to run the time then. He must place the pieces without they superposing one.

In the Childlike level, the pieces place one in his position automatically when they are established in the figure outline.

He can stop the game cliking on the name of other figure or good on the first itme of the figure tree (with that name of the form and of the level current).

Making the program to suggest a figure at random is possible (menu Game->Suggest figure or good with the combination of Ctrl+P keys.) Also looking for a figure by his name is possible (menu Game->Search figure name or good with the combination of Ctrl+N keys.)

He can require help of the program with the menu options Game->Places piece (the program will place the piece cliked) and with the option Game ->View solution (view the solution in the game zone.) The two options deduct points.

If he manages to solve the figure, the program will indicate it showing the outline in green, one he will update the counter and he will stop the clock. The program keeps your solution: just click on the figure name to view your solution. You can remove the solution with a double click. The punctuation of each figure depends on the form of the game and of the figure difficulty. Three levels distinguish one from difficulty indicated by the point of green (easier), yellow (medium) and red (difficult) color.
