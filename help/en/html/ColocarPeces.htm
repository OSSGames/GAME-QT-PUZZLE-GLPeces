He helps the figure solving

The program offers two kinds of help in the solving of her figures: Showing the solution (Game-> View the solution) or placing the pieces individually.

The last option is accessible in the menu option Game->Places piece. Once activated the menu option, to the click on a piece, this will place one in his position. Click on each one of the pieces, will manage to obtain the solution.

If he uses this help option, the program will deduct the proportional part of the punctuation by each one of the placed pieces.
